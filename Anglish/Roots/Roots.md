# Roots
## prim

Latin first

- primary
- primitive
- primeval
	- origin in history
- primal
	- basic or primitive (animal nature)
- primer
	- a small book for children
	- a small introductory book on a subject
- primate
- primordial

## homo

same

- homograph
- homosexual
- homonym
- homogeneous
- homogenize
- homologous

## dis

Basic meaning: apart
Extended meaning: opposite or not(distaste, disagreeable), deprive of(disinfect), exclude or expel from(disbar)

- dissuade
- disorient
- discredit
- dislodge
