# Exasperated

## Pronunciation

- ɪgˈzæspəreɪtɪd

## Meanings

### ADJ-GRADED

If you describe a person as exasperated, you mean that they are frustrated or angry because of something that is happening or something that another person is doing.  

> Bertha was exasperated at the delay...
