# Chortle

## Pronunciation

- ˈtʃɔ:tl
- ˈtʃɔrtl

## Meanings

### VERB

To chortle means to laugh in a way that shows you are very pleased.  

> There was silence for a moment, then Larry began chortling like an idiot.



## Therusaus

laugh, chuckle
